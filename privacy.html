Privacy Policy

This is a placeholder privacy template for Hungter beta. Have a licensed lawyer review before production launch.

1. Data We Collect

  • Browser localStorage usage data (messages count, streak, level, subjects explored, quiz scores, milestones).
  • Visitor/session identifiers sent to backend APIs for usage analytics.
  • Chat messages submitted by users.

2. How Data Is Processed

Chat content can be sent to third-party AI providers including Groq, OpenAI, and Anthropic for generating educational responses.

3. Payments

Hungter does not currently operate finalized real-money production billing. Checkout and payment flows are in beta/testing mode.

4. Storage and Retention

Most learner progress is stored in your browser localStorage. Server analytics may retain aggregate stats such as daily chat counts and subject counts.

5. Beta Disclaimer

Hungter is an evolving beta product and privacy practices may be updated as infrastructure matures.

6. Contact

For privacy requests, contact: privacy@hungter.com
