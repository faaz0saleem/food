FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is MindMesh?

MindMesh is an AI tutor that adapts explanations to your learning style using multiple AI engines.

Is it free?

There is a free beta plan. Paid plans are being tested and may change before launch.

Which AIs power it?

MindMesh can route chats through Groq, OpenAI, and Anthropic engines depending on configuration and question type.

Is my data safe?

Most progress data is saved in your browser localStorage. Chat prompts may be sent to AI providers to generate responses.

How does the XP system work?

XP is weighted using messages, subject variety, quiz participation, quiz accuracy, streak consistency, and milestones.

How do I reset my progress?

Open your profile page and use the "Reset all progress" button in the danger zone.
