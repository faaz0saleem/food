Our Story

Hungter was built by someone who did not love studying either.

Hungter started with one annoying problem: most explanations in school felt like they were written for someone else. The "official" explanation might be correct, but it did not always make sense in the way my brain worked. I kept wishing for a tutor that could rephrase things until it clicked.

So at 15, I started building one. Not because I was the perfect student, and definitely not because I had everything figured out, but because I wanted learning to feel less frustrating and more human.

From experiment to product

At first, Hungter was just a personal experiment: one chat box, rough UI, and lots of bugs. Then friends started trying it, and the same feedback kept showing up: "This actually explains it in my language." That is when it stopped being a side project and started becoming a real product.

What we believe

People do not fail because they are "bad at learning." They fail because the explanation format does not match how they think. Hungter exists to close that gap with multiple tutoring styles, adaptive guidance, and progress tools that reward consistency over perfection.

We are still in beta, still improving, and still building this with the same original goal: make hard ideas finally make sense for real students.
