Pricing questions

Is Hungter really free?

Yes. Every new account gets $3 of AI credits — about 1,500 messages — that never expire. No card, no trial countdown. When you run out, you can upgrade or keep using the non-AI study tools.

What does "$3 of AI" mean?

Instead of a confusing token count, we show the real AI budget. $3 covers roughly 1,500 tutor messages. Student and Pro top that up with $8 and $30 of AI every month.

What can paid plans do that Free can't?

Student adds the ⚡ All-4 compare mode (four AI answers side by side) and priority generation. Pro adds Solvable Guess Papers and Codex, our AI app builder, plus early access to new features.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — plans are monthly and you can cancel whenever you like. You keep access until the end of the period.
